RapidDirect Manufacturing Services

With our own factory in Shenzhen and a strong network of vetted manufacturers across China, we offer a broad range of manufacturing capabilities — from CNC machining to injection molding, sheet metal, and 3D printing. Whether you need fast-turn prototypes or low- to high-volume production, we're your trusted partner for complex, custom parts delivered with speed and precision.

CNC Machining

CNC milling, turning & precision machining for prototypes and end-use parts. ±0.001" tolerance, 0.020" thin walls, 1-day lead times.

Injection Molding

Get plastic injection molding, overmolding & insert molding with fast & production tooling. No MOQ. T1 samples in 2 weeks. Scale 1 to 1M units.

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Expert laser cutting, bending, welding & stamping. Fast, precise sheet metal prototypes to production. 3-day lead time. 0.008 in. thin walls.

3D Printing

SLA, HP MJF, SLS & SLM for functional prototypes & low-volume 3D printing. ±0.008 in. precision. 1-day lead time.
