Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

What are your typical turnaround times?

expand_more

Turnaround times vary depending on the complexity and volume of the order. Standard prototyping can take as little as 3-5 business days, while high-volume production runs are quoted on a case-by-case basis. Expedited options are available.

What file formats do you accept for quotes?

expand_more

We accept a wide range of 3D CAD file formats, including STEP, IGES, STL, SLDPRT, and X_T. For the most accurate quotes, we recommend providing a STEP file along with a 2D drawing in PDF format if available.

What materials can you work with?

expand_more

Our capabilities include a vast array of materials, from various plastics like ABS, PC, and Nylon to metals such as Aluminum, Stainless Steel, and Titanium. Please visit our Materials page for a complete list and specifications.

Do you offer international shipping?

expand_more

Yes, we ship parts to customers worldwide. We partner with major logistics providers to ensure reliable and timely delivery. Shipping costs and times will be included in your formal quotation.
